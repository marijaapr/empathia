How Empathia Works

Reflect on your own with AI, or connect with psychology students building real-world experience under supervision.

  1. 1

    Create your account

    Sign up as someone seeking support, or as a psychology student offering supervised practice sessions.

  2. 2

    Chat with AI or a student

    Use the AI companion anytime for reflection and mood tracking, or request a session with a student psychologist when you want human connection.

  3. 3

    Track your progress

    Log how you feel over time and continue conversations in a private space designed for emotional support.

Student psychologists on this platform are in training and are not licensed clinicians. Empathia is not for emergencies—please contact local crisis services if you need immediate help.
